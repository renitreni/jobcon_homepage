How to Avail Our Services

Foreign Employer Shall Submit to the Job Connections International Manpower Services, Inc. the following documents for accreditation with the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA):
Documents we need from your company for processing and accreditation with our labor department:
  • Special Power of Attorney
  • Recruitment Agreement
  • Authority to recruitment foreign labor from the council for visa certificate for Middle east employers.
  • Employment Contract
  • Company License
  • Manpower Request

Note: Documents must have verification and sealed from Philippine overseas Labor office (POLO) and Philippine Embassy.
